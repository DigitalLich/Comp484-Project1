The World

The world in itself is divided in 6 continents, with several diferent nations and biomes in each one of them. The names of them are still being written and/or haven't been discovered by players forgotten by time. However, the one continent that has been brewing in livelihood is Eusacix.

Eusacix

Eusacix is the continent where the main story is being told. It is a continent with a lot of diversity in terms of biomes, with a huge wasteland on the southeast, mega forests, 2 chains of mountains, several plains, and a tundra on the east.

The nations that are known to exist in Eusacix are:

  • The Holy Sovereignty of Dhavalon
  • The Erohttus Coalition
  • The Ghivernna Kingdom
  • The Hestein Empire
  • The Principality of Kahla'gar
  • The Krigdalthar Empire
  • The Lhanersk Palatinate
  • The Nyuldher Coalition of Marshlands
  • The Phenbora Dominion
  • The Solthios Wasteland

Each nation has its own culture, traditions and history. The Kingdom of Aentis is known for its chivalry and honor, while the Empire of Drakoria is known for its military prowess and expansionism. The Republic of Valtoria is known for its democracy and trade, while the Confederation of Nordheim is known for its ruggedness and resilience. The Sultanate of Zafaria is known for its wealth and opulence, while the Duchy of Eldoria is known for its art and architecture.
