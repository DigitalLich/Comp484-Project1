Bordin Griv

Class: Fighter
Ancestry: Human
Age: 42
Personality: Hardworking, Protective, Thoughtful, and Messy.
Relationships: A wife and a daughter.
Background: Fisherman
Life: He grew up in a small house near the seashore. His father (Galio) disappeared, and he was raised by his mother and his maternal grandfather (Virdar), who taught him how to fish. From a young age, he was always a hard worker. He gained his combat experience by talking to soldiers who often passed by his boat while he was fishing, giving them advice or teaching them how to use a sword. Bordin helped his grandfather sell the fish they caught to a merchant who came to their house in a cart. This merchant was accompanied by his daughter, who would later become his wife, Silin. Silin left her father's house to live with Bordin, and they later had a daughter named Kehna.
Values: A wooden carved figure made by his daughter.
Worships: Zemera
Fears: Fear of the unknown.
Relaxes by: Listening to the waves of the sea and fishing.
